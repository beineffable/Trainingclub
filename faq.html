Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Training Club

What membership options do you offer?

We offer two membership tiers:

  • Standard Membership: Access to all regular classes at your home location
  • Elite Membership: Access to all classes at all locations, priority booking, and exclusive events

Both tiers are available as monthly subscriptions, term packages, or annual memberships. You can view all options on our memberships page.

How do I join Training Club?

Training Club is an exclusive fitness community that operates on a referral basis. To join, you'll need an access code from a current member or directly from our team. Once you have your code, you can register through our registration page.

Can I pause my membership when I'm on holiday?

Yes! We offer a holiday pause feature that allows you to temporarily suspend your membership when you're away. You can pause for up to 28 days per year. This can be managed through your member profile.

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of outdoor training sessions including:

  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
  • Strength & Conditioning
  • Mobility & Recovery
  • Endurance Training
  • Specialized sessions (trail running, hill sprints, etc.)

All classes are conducted outdoors in various weather conditions, which is a core part of our training philosophy.

Do you train in all weather conditions?

Yes! Training in all weather conditions is a fundamental aspect of our approach. We believe that challenging yourself in different environments builds both physical and mental resilience.

We train in rain, snow, heat, and cold. The only exceptions are during severe weather warnings or dangerous conditions (lightning, extreme storms, etc.) where safety would be compromised.

What should I bring to a training session?

For every session, you should bring:

  • Water bottle
  • Small towel
  • Appropriate footwear (trail running shoes recommended)
  • Weather-appropriate clothing (layers are best)
  • Your Training Club membership card or app

We provide all necessary equipment for the workout.

How do I book a class?

You can book classes through:

  • Our mobile app (recommended)
  • The online booking portal
  • Calling your local Training Club location

Bookings open 7 days in advance and close 1 hour before the class starts.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel a booking up to 12 hours before the class starts without penalty. Cancellations made less than 12 hours before the class will incur a late cancellation fee of 10 CHF or the deduction of one class credit.

No-shows (failing to attend without cancellation) will result in a 20 CHF fee or the deduction of two class credits.

Can I join a waiting list if a class is full?

Yes, we maintain waiting lists for all classes. If a spot becomes available, the system will automatically notify the next person on the list. You'll have 2 hours to confirm your spot before it's offered to the next person.

Elite members receive priority on waiting lists.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • TWINT
  • Bank transfer
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay (in-app only)

All recurring memberships require a valid payment method on file.

How do I update my payment information?

You can update your payment information through your member profile under the "Payment Methods" section. For security reasons, some changes may require verification.

Do you offer any discounts?

We offer the following discounts:

  • 10% off annual memberships
  • 15% family discount when 2+ members join from the same household
  • Corporate rates for companies with 5+ employees joining
  • Seasonal promotions (announced via email and social media)

Discounts cannot be combined. Contact us for more information about eligibility.
