GENERAL QUESTIONS

What is TRAINING CLUB?

TRAINING CLUB is a premium outdoor bootcamp fitness program operated by Ineffable GmbH in Switzerland. We deliver physically and mentally transformative group fitness using elite equipment and structured coaching in all weather conditions, year-round.

Our unique mobile setup allows us to bring a complete gym environment to various outdoor locations, providing a fresh and challenging training experience that can't be found in traditional gyms.

What makes TRAINING CLUB different from other bootcamps?

TRAINING CLUB stands apart from other bootcamps in several key ways:

  • Premium Equipment: We bring a fully-equipped mobile gym to every session, including squat racks, pull-up bars, kettlebells, barbells, and TRXs.
  • All-Weather Training: We train outdoors year-round, regardless of weather conditions, building mental resilience alongside physical strength.
  • Structured Programming: Our sessions follow a carefully designed 90-minute structure with warm-up, circuit training, mental conditioning "burner" finisher, and cooldown.
  • Community Focus: We foster a tight-knit community of like-minded individuals who enjoy working hard, having fun, and challenging themselves.
  • Exclusive Membership: Our access-code system ensures our community consists of motivated individuals who are referred by existing members.
Who can join TRAINING CLUB?

TRAINING CLUB welcomes anyone who wants to challenge themselves and improve their fitness, regardless of their starting point. Our members range from motivated beginners to experienced athletes.

The only requirements are:

  • You must have an access code (obtained through our referral system)
  • A positive attitude and willingness to push yourself
  • Commitment to respecting our community values

Our coaches are skilled at scaling exercises to accommodate different fitness levels, ensuring everyone gets an appropriate challenge.

How do I get started?

To get started with TRAINING CLUB:

  1. Obtain an Access Code: You'll need a referral code from an existing member or directly from our team.
  2. Register: Use your access code to create an account on our website.
  3. Choose Your Membership: Select from our various membership options.
  4. Book Your First Session: Use our online booking system to reserve your spot.
  5. Show Up: Arrive 10 minutes early for your first session to meet your coach and get oriented.

If you don't have an access code but are interested in joining, you can contact us directly, and we'll connect you with a current member who can provide a referral.

MEMBERSHIP QUESTIONS

  • Standard Membership: 100 CHF access fee (requires access code)
  • Session Pricing: 35 CHF per session
  • 10-Session Package: 330 CHF (6-month validity)
  • 20-Session Package: 630 CHF (5% discount, 12-month validity)

  • Unlimited Membership: 450 CHF/month (unlimited sessions, 3-month commitment)

We also offer Elite membership options with additional benefits for our most dedicated members. Contact us for details about Elite membership.

What is the access code system?

Our access code system is designed to maintain the quality and community aspect of TRAINING CLUB:

  • Standard Codes: These are distributed by existing members through our referral system (e.g., TC-KNONAU-2025).
  • Purpose: The system ensures our community grows through trusted connections and maintains a high level of commitment and motivation among members.
  • Obtaining a Code: You can get an access code by being referred by a current member or by contacting us directly if you don't know any current members.
  • Usage: Each code can only be used once and expires after a certain period.

This referral-based growth helps us maintain our unique training atmosphere and community spirit.

How does the referral system work?

Our referral system rewards members for growing our community:

  • Code Distribution: Members can request up to 3 standard access codes per quarter.
  • Rewards: Each successful referral (someone who joins using your code) earns you a session credit or free term extension.
  • Tracking: You can monitor your referrals and rewards through your member dashboard.
  • Purpose: This system helps us grow organically through trusted connections while rewarding our members for their support.

The referral system is one of the ways we maintain our community's quality while providing additional value to our loyal members.

What happens if I need to pause my membership?

We understand that life sometimes requires flexibility:

  • Holiday Flexibility: Credits remain valid during holidays, and we can extend expiration windows by 1-3 weeks upon request.
  • Membership Freeze: Monthly and unlimited memberships can be frozen for up to 4 weeks per year with advance notice.
  • Medical Reasons: For medical issues requiring extended breaks, we offer customized solutions - just contact us with documentation.
  • Automatic Holiday Adjustments: During official Swiss holiday periods, you can opt for automatic membership freezing.

Our goal is to accommodate reasonable flexibility while maintaining the commitment that makes our community special.

TRAINING QUESTIONS

What is the structure of a typical session?

Each TRAINING CLUB session follows a carefully designed 90-minute structure:

  1. Warm-up (15 minutes): Dynamic mobility work and movement preparation to prepare the body for the workout.
  2. Circuit Training (60 minutes): 6-station circuit with a mix of strength, conditioning, and skill work. Each station is coached with proper technique instruction.
  3. Burner Finisher (10 minutes): High-intensity challenge designed to push mental limits and build resilience.
  4. Cooldown (5 minutes): Mobility work and stretching to aid recovery.

This structure ensures a comprehensive workout that develops strength, endurance, and mental toughness while minimizing injury risk through proper preparation and recovery.

What is the "Burner" and why is it important?

The "Burner" is a signature element of TRAINING CLUB sessions and serves as a mental conditioning highlight:

  • Format: A high-intensity, challenging finisher that pushes you beyond your perceived limits.
  • Purpose: To develop mental resilience, grit, and the ability to perform under fatigue - skills that transfer to all areas of life.
  • Variety: Burners change regularly and might include AMRAP (As Many Rounds As Possible), EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute), or team challenges.
  • Community: The shared experience of completing challenging burners creates strong bonds among members.

While physically demanding, the real benefit of the Burner is psychological - teaching you that you're capable of more than you think and building confidence that extends beyond the training session.

Do you train in all weather conditions?

Yes, we train outdoors year-round in all weather conditions, which is a core part of our philosophy:

  • Rain or Shine: Sessions continue in rain, snow, heat, or cold - we adapt the workout as needed for safety.
  • Mental Benefits: Training in challenging conditions builds mental toughness and resilience that transfers to all areas of life.
  • Preparation: We provide guidance on appropriate clothing and preparation for different weather conditions.
  • Safety First: In extreme weather conditions (severe storms, dangerous winds), we may modify or reschedule sessions for safety.

Our all-weather approach is one of the things that makes TRAINING CLUB unique and effective. There's something special about completing a challenging workout in the rain or snow that builds character in ways indoor training simply cannot match.

What is the annual training calendar?

Our annual training calendar is divided into four distinct terms, each with specific focus areas:

  • Term 1: Strength & Athletic Base (Jan–Apr)
    Focus on building foundational strength and athletic capacity after the holiday season.
  • Term 2: Athletic Performance & Fat Loss (May–Jul)
    Emphasis on explosive power, speed, and metabolic conditioning for summer readiness.
  • Term 3: Hypertrophy & Work Capacity (Aug–Oct)
    Concentration on muscle development and increasing overall work capacity.
  • Term 4: Mental Fortitude & Peak Strength (Oct–Dec)
    Challenging sessions focused on mental toughness and achieving peak strength before year-end.

Each term includes custom programming and periodic assessments to track progress. This structured approach ensures balanced development throughout the year and prevents training plateaus.

I'm a beginner. Is TRAINING CLUB too advanced for me?

TRAINING CLUB is designed to be challenging but accessible to all fitness levels, including beginners:

  • Scalable Exercises: All movements can be modified to match your current ability level while still providing an appropriate challenge.
  • Coached Sessions: Our trainers provide individual attention to ensure proper form and appropriate intensity.
  • Supportive Community: Our members are encouraging and remember their own starting points.
  • Progressive Development: We focus on building skills and strength gradually over time.

The most important qualities for success at TRAINING CLUB are not your current fitness level, but rather your attitude, willingness to learn, and commitment to showing up consistently. Many of our most dedicated members started as complete beginners.

EQUIPMENT QUESTIONS

What equipment do you use in sessions?

TRAINING CLUB utilizes a comprehensive range of premium fitness equipment that we transport to each location via our mobile trailer setup:

  • Strength Equipment: Squat racks, barbells, weight plates, dumbbells, kettlebells
  • Functional Training: TRX suspension trainers, battle ropes, sandbags, medicine balls
  • Conditioning Tools: Assault bikes, rowing machines, sleds, jump ropes
  • Mobility Equipment: Foam rollers, resistance bands, yoga mats
  • Specialized Gear: Plyo boxes, agility ladders, cones, gymnastics rings

This extensive equipment selection allows us to create varied, challenging workouts that develop all aspects of fitness while keeping sessions fresh and engaging.

How does the mobile trailer concept work?

Our mobile trailer concept is what makes TRAINING CLUB unique:

  • Custom-Built Trailer: We've designed a specialized trailer that efficiently stores and transports all our premium equipment.
  • Quick Setup: Our team arrives before each session to transform the outdoor space into a complete gym environment.
  • Location Flexibility: The mobile concept allows us to train in various locations while maintaining consistent equipment quality.
  • All-Weather Design: Our equipment is selected and maintained to perform in all weather conditions.
  • Full Gym Experience: Despite being outdoors, you'll have access to the same quality equipment you'd find in a premium gym.

This mobile approach combines the benefits of outdoor training with the equipment variety of a fully-equipped gym, creating a unique training experience that can't be found elsewhere.

What should I bring to a session?

For a successful TRAINING CLUB session, we recommend bringing:

  • Water Bottle: Stay hydrated throughout the session.
  • Appropriate Clothing: Comfortable workout clothes suitable for the weather conditions. Layers are recommended in cooler months.
  • Training Shoes: Supportive athletic shoes with good traction for outdoor surfaces.
  • Small Towel: For personal use during the workout.
  • Positive Attitude: The most important thing to bring!

Optional items that some members find helpful:

  • Lifting gloves (for hand protection)
  • Heart rate monitor
  • Light rain jacket (in uncertain weather)
  • Post-workout snack

We provide all the necessary equipment for the workout itself, so you don't need to bring any fitness gear.

What happens in case of extreme weather?

While we train in most weather conditions, safety is our priority:

  • Normal Conditions: Rain, snow, cold, and heat are all considered normal training conditions, and sessions proceed as scheduled with appropriate modifications.
  • Extreme Weather: In cases of severe lightning storms, dangerous winds, or other safety hazards, we may modify or reschedule sessions.
  • Notification System: If a session needs to be modified or rescheduled, we'll notify all registered participants at least 2 hours in advance via email and text message.
  • Credits: If we need to cancel a session due to extreme conditions, your credit will be automatically returned to your account.

Weather-related modifications are rare, as training in challenging conditions is part of what makes TRAINING CLUB unique and effective for building mental resilience.
