Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Training Club

What membership options do you offer?

We offer two membership tiers:

  • Standard Membership: Access to all regular classes at your home location
  • Elite Membership: Access to all classes at all locations, priority booking, and exclusive events

Both tiers are available as monthly subscriptions, term packages, or annual memberships. You can view all options on our memberships page.

How do I join Training Club?

Training Club is an exclusive fitness community that operates on a referral basis. To join, you'll need an access code from a current member or directly from our team. Once you have your code, you can register through our registration page.

Can I pause my membership when I'm on holiday?

Yes! We offer a holiday pause feature that allows you to temporarily suspend your membership when you're away. You can pause for up to 28 days per year. This can be managed through your member profile.

What types of classes do you offer?

We offer a variety of outdoor fitness classes including:

  • Morning Bootcamp
  • HIIT Sessions
  • Strength & Conditioning
  • Mobility & Recovery
  • Weekend Warrior Workouts

Check our class schedule for the full lineup and availability.

What happens if it rains?

Training Club operates in all weather conditions! Our motto is "Elite outdoor training in all weather conditions." We believe training outdoors in various weather builds mental and physical resilience. We only cancel classes in extreme weather conditions where safety is a concern.

How do I book a class?

Booking a class is easy! Simply log in to your member account, go to the class schedule, select your preferred class, and click the "Book" button. You'll receive an email confirmation of your booking.

How far in advance can I book classes?

Standard members can book classes up to 7 days in advance. Elite members enjoy priority booking and can book classes up to 14 days in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel a class booking up to 12 hours before the scheduled start time without penalty. Cancellations made less than 12 hours in advance will result in the class credit being deducted from your account. You can manage your bookings through your member profile.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including:

  • TWINT
  • PayPal
  • Klarna
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)

All payments are processed securely through our payment provider.

How do I manage my payment information?

You can manage your payment information through your member profile. This includes updating your payment method, viewing your billing history, and managing your subscription details.

What is the credit system?

Our credit system allows you to book classes using credits allocated to your account. Monthly members receive a set number of credits each month, while package members have a fixed number of credits for their term. You can track your credit usage in your member profile.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us.
